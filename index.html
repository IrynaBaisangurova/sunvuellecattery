About us

We are the Gordeev family

My name is Tatyana Gordeeva. I live in Ukraine together with my husband and daughter. I have been breeding Persian cats for over 10 years. Our pets are part of our friendly family. They receive all our love and care, which they need.

We test the health of all our cats before they are used in breeding. Our cats are health tested for HCM and PKD.

We want our cats to be within what you would expect from a Persian breed. That is why we attend shows to be evaluated by international judges.

The Persian cat is one of the most beautiful of all domestic breeds. It is a true aristocrat who incredibly combines unmatched looks, intelligence, and regal demeanor with amazing affection and sincere love for its owner. Due to this harmonious combination Persian cat is confidently ahead of other breeds in the popularity rating.

You can see if we have kittens under "Kittens". If you are interested, please contact me at the contact information listed on the website. Thank you for your visit!
